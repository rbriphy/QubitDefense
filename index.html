How to Play

• Enemies exist in quantum superposition |ψ⟩ = α|0⟩ + β|1⟩

• Place quantum gates to rotate the enemy's state on the Bloch sphere

• Use Measurement towers to collapse the state:

- Z-Measure: collapses to |0⟩ (survive) or |1⟩ (die)

- X-Measure: collapses to |+⟩ (die) or |-⟩ (survive)

- Y-Measure: collapses to |↗⟩ (die) or |↙⟩ (survive)

• Build towers along the path to kill enemies before they escape!

• Left-click to place towers, right-click to remove
